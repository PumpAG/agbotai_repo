Features

Market Analysis

Scans real-time data from APIs of major exchanges like Binance, KuCoin, and Bybit. Detects unusual spikes in trading volume and buying activity in low-cap tokens. Uses machine learning models to identify early-stage pump patterns.

Social Media Monitoring

Analyzes trends on Twitter, Telegram, Reddit, and Discord using Natural Language Processing (NLP). Detects unusual mentions of projects and correlates social activity with price movements.

Real-Time Alerts Generation

When a token shows signs of a potential pump, the AI sends an instant alert to Telegram and Twitter. The alert includes key data: Token name and ticker, volume growth percentage and absolute values, market cap updates, price evolution, and trend source (Twitter mentions, Telegram activity, trading volume, etc.).

Continuous Monitoring & Updates

Keeps traders informed with automatic updates at regular intervals. Evaluates if the trend remains bullish or shows signs of manipulation.
