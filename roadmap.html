Roadmap

Q1 2025

The project begins with team formation and feasibility studies. The official website is developed with all essential features, and the staking system is launched with a 25% APR. The Fairlaunch takes place on PinkSale, accompanied by a marketing strategy and influencer collaborations. The NFT collection is introduced at 0.25 BNB each, followed by listings on CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, and initial exchange listings.

Q2 2025

The Token Creator tool is launched on the BNB network, allowing users to create tokens without technical knowledge. An automated volume bot is implemented to enhance token visibility, and advanced tracking tools for tokens and wallets are integrated. The NFT collection expands with new staking benefits.

Q3 2025

The Token Booster is deployed, providing visibility through internal promotions and strategic partnerships. An automated smart contract auditing system is introduced, alongside a competitor analysis tool that provides market insights. Community management tools are also launched, offering private groups and customized communication channels.

Q4 2025

Integration with major news platforms such as CoinMarketCap and Binance Square ensures increased project exposure. An automated news distribution system is implemented, while the rewards system is expanded to encourage user engagement. The full ecosystem is deployed, activating all platform functionalities for a complete user experience.
